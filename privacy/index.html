Privacy Policy

ScriptFlow does not upload, store, or transmit any audio or video file you select. All transcription happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly and an on-device speech recognition model. Your files never leave your device.

The speech recognition model itself (roughly 40–75MB) is downloaded once from a public CDN and cached by your browser so future transcriptions work offline. This download contains no information about you or your files.

This site may show advertising via Google AdSense, which can use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can control ad personalization in yourGoogle Ads Settings.

We do not use analytics that identify individual users, and we do not sell or share any data because we do not collect any file content in the first place.

Every transcription runs locally in your browser. Nothing you upload is ever sent to a server.
