About ScriptFlow

ScriptFlow is a single, focused tool: turning audio and video into text. It runs entirely in your web browser using WebAssembly and an on-device machine learning model — no file you upload is ever sent to a server.

Because there is no server-side processing, ScriptFlow can stay completely free with no account, no watermark, and no file-size games. The site is supported by on-page advertising.

Longer files can take longer to transcribe on older devices since the work happens on your own hardware rather than in the cloud.

Every transcription runs locally in your browser. Nothing you upload is ever sent to a server.
